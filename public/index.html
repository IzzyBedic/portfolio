Who am I?

I am a big nature-lover! I never missed a year of farm camp from pre-K to a high school senior; something about sleeping outside made waking up at 4am to start chores fun. I have also backpacked a couple sections of the PCT! A large part of my motivation for a career in data science is the ability to see what is happening to the natural world that I grew up loving and know is being threatened. I hope to contribute to scientific discoveries and policy changes to help save the outdoors and its constituents.
